Explore New Places

Jaipur

The Pink City

It is a vibrant blend of history and modernity in Rajasthan. Famous for its stunning architecture, bustling bazaars, and colorful culture, it's a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a taste of India's rich heritage.

Jaisalmer

The Golden City

It is a mesmerizing destination in Rajasthan, India. It boasts the stunning Jaisalmer Fort, intricately carved havelis, vibrant bazaars, and captivating desert landscapes. Famous for camel safaris, it offers a rich cultural experience against the backdrop of golden sands.

Udaipur

The City of Lakes

It is renowned for its stunning palaces, serene lakes, and romantic ambiance. It's a captivating destination that bewitches visitors with its rich history and picturesque beauty.Udaipur's timeless charm and regal splendor make it a truly unforgettable destination in the heart of Rajasthan.

Hotels

Rambagh Palace

Regal and romantic atmosphere with grandeur and elegance, featuring beautiful gardens, roaming peacocks, and flower-patterned rooms. Make sure to visit this gateway.

Hotel Lakend

Scenic lakeside hotel offering stunning views and fantastic hospitality. Spacious rooms with mesmerizing lake vistas. On-site restaurant serving delicious, authentic cuisine in a refreshing ambiance.

Trident Udaipur

Breathtaking Lake Pichola location offering stunning views, beautiful gardens, and easy lake access. Exceptional hospitality, comfortable rooms, and memorable boat rides.

Ramee Royal Resorts & Spa Udaipur

Nature lover's paradise with stunning mountain views, serene location, and tranquil atmosphere. Luxurious rooms with private pool options. Enjoy live singing shows, delicious food, and exceptional hospitality.

The LaLit Jaipur

Well-maintained hotel near airport and city center with friendly staff and clean premises. Praises for housekeeping and front office teams. On-site Baluchi restaurant offers exceptional food and ambiance. Have a visit!
